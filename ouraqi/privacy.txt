Privacy Policy

        Last updated: February 26, 2026

        1. Information We Access
        OuraQi accesses health and personal data from your Oura account via the Oura API, including sleep, activity, readiness, stress, resilience, workout, tag, and personal information.

        2. Data Storage
        Your Oura API token is stored locally on your device using the app's secure storage. No health data is stored on external servers or transmitted to third parties.

        3. Data Sharing
        We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal or health data to any third parties. Data remains on your device.

        4. Analytics
        OuraQi does not collect analytics, usage data, or tracking information.

        5. Third-Party Services
        OuraQi connects to the Oura API (api.ouraring.com) to retrieve your data. Please refer to Oura's privacy policy for information on how Oura handles your data.

        6. Your Rights
        You can revoke OuraQi's access to your Oura data at any time by signing out within the app or revoking access in your Oura account settings.

        7. Contact
        For questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the app's support channels.
